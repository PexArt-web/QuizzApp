Quiz Instructions
The quizzes consists of questions carefully designed to help you self-assess your comprehension. No data will be collected on the website regarding your responses or how many times you take the quiz.
Each question in the quiz is of multiple-choice format. Read each question carefully, and click on the input button of your choice. Each correct or incorrect response will result in appropriate feedback immediately at the bottom of the screen.
After responding to a question, click on the "Next" button at the bottom to go to the next question. After responding
to all questions, click on "Submit" below to display your result.
If you select an incorrect response for a question, you can try again until you get the correct response. The total score for the quiz is based on your responses to all questions. If you respond incorrectly to a question or retake a
question again and get the correct response, your quiz score will reflect it appropriately. However, your quiz will not be
graded, if you skip a question or exit before responding to all the questions.
Note: Your TIME STARTS immediately after the first question ,you have a maximum of 10 minutes on all the questions
